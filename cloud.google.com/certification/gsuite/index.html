Google Cloud Certified

G Suite

G Suite

As the workplace becomes increasingly digital, collaborating effectively using real-time productivity tools is becoming a prerequisite skill. A Google Cloud certification in G Suite signals to employers your readiness to operate in today's modern work environment. By earning the G Suite certification, you prove your ability to use cloud-based tools to create and share documents, spreadsheets, presentations, and files. You demonstrate that you can communicate effectively with email and online meeting solutions.

The Google Cloud Certified - G Suite exam verifies proficiency in key features of the G Suite platform:

  • check Drive
  • check Gmail
  • check Hangouts Meet
  • check Docs
  • check Sheets
  • check Slides

About this certification exam

This exam objectively measures an individual's ability to work, communicate, and manage tasks using G Suite’s comprehensive productivity and collaboration tools in the workplace. To earn this certification you must pass the G Suite certification exam.

This exam is remotely proctored and administered online in English. It has no prerequisites. The format is multiple choice and performance-based. The G Suite certification exam is taken in a live G Suite domain specifically created for the exam. You’ll be given a series of tasks to perform using G Suite for the performance-based section.

  • Length: 2 hours
  • Registration fee: USD 75
  • System Requirements: Working webcam and microphone; latest version of Chrome.
  • Note: After registration, within 24-48 hours you will receive further instructions for how to complete the exam.
