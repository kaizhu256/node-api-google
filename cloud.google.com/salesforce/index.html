We’re teaming up to enable smarter, more collaborative experiences for our customers. And, as an added bonus, Salesforce customers will be eligible for G Suite licenses at no cost for up to one year. See details.

Google & Salesforce bring the power of cloud to businesses globally

New strategic partnership means better experiences for customers.

  • G Suite: Real-time collaboration and higher productivity with CRM and G Suite
  • Google Cloud Platform: Salesforce has named Google Cloud as a preferred public cloud provider and will use Google Cloud Platform for international infrastructure expansion of its core services
  • Google Analytics 360: Complete insights about customer journeys to drive smarter engagement

Read our blog post to learn more.

Connect G Suite & Salesforce

Salesforce Lightning for Gmail:

Users can surface relevant Salesforce CRM data in Gmail, and interactions from Gmail directly within Salesforce.

Salesforce Lightning for Google Sheets:

Users will be able to auto-update data between Salesforce Records or Reports and Google Sheets.

Quip Live Apps for Google Drive and Google Calendar:

Teams will be able to embed and access Drive files (e.g., Google Docs, Slides, Sheets) or their Google Calendar inside Quip.

Salesforce for Hangouts Meet:

Within Hangouts Meet, users will be able to surface relevant Salesforce customer and account details, service case history, and more.

To help Salesforce customers take advantage of these new innovations, Google will offer Salesforce customers G Suite licenses at no cost for up to one year. The promotion is available to eligible Salesforce customers who are new to G Suite. Restrictions apply. See details.

G Suite customers receive an exclusive offer for Salesforce Essentials, free for three months from Salesforce. Restrictions may apply. See details.

Google Analytics 360 & Salesforce

New Salesforce and Google Analytics 360 integrations will seamlessly connect sales, marketing, and advertising data across Salesforce Sales Cloud, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and Google Analytics 360 for the first time. G Suite Promotional Offer for Salesforce Customers

Try G Suite with Salesforce

Google Cloud is offering eligible Salesforce customers who are new to G Suite the opportunity to receive G Suite seats at no cost for up to one (1) year at the time of purchase through Salesforce. Starting on November 6, 2017, Salesforce customers who are new to G Suite are eligible for G Suite services for a promotional period as part of their Salesforce order. Promotional offer ends on December 31, 2018.

Number of Employees G Suite SKU Promotional Period
0-9 Basic 3 months
10-349 Business 3 months
350+ Business 1 year

Terms and conditions

The number of eligible G Suite seats will be at least equal to the number of Salesforce seats and up to the whole domain. Actual number of seats offered for the promotion is at the sole discretion of Google.

Offer is valid only for new G Suite customers that have not purchased G Suite in the past twenty-four (24) months.

Promotional offer is available in countries where G Suite is offered.

Offer is available only to Salesforce customers and through Salesforce.

Salesforce customers who are new to G Suite may also be eligible for other Google promotions. Google promotional offers cannot be combined.

Promotional offer is limited to one per company.

The promotional offer is open to government customers assuming there are no regulatory constraints.

G Suite services will be activated by Google when the designated customer administrator proves ownership of the company domain that will be used with the G Suite services and accepts the standard G Suite Terms of Service via the G Suite Admin Console. The promotional period starts on the date the G Suite services are activated. Customer administrators can add/remove G Suite services via the G Suite Admin Console.

At the end of the promotional period, customer will be charged standard list price for the continued use of G Suite services. The promotional offer offered cannot be renewed.

Customers must set up payment in the G Suite Admin Console to continue use of G Suite Services after the end of the promotional period.

To redeem the offer, contact Salesforce.

Offer has no cash value and cannot be sold or otherwise bartered.

Void where prohibited by law. This offer is subject to all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations.
