Education Solutions

Enable new research, help secure your critical data, and overcome learning barriers with our flexible technology and next-gen productivity tools.

Higher Education

Power what’s possible in teaching, research, IT, and entrepreneurship.

To keep up with increasing demands on infrastructure, manage growing data archives, and empower future computer scientists, many higher education institutions are moving to Google Cloud. Google Cloud Platform (GCP) runs on the same infrastructure and private fiber network that Google uses, giving you the flexibility and power you need to store and analyze your most critical data. Help your researchers move from bold ideas to big discoveries faster with our Big Data tools. Encourage students to advance their skills with our range of computer science opportunities and our open, innovative tools like TensorFlow and App Engine. Enable multi-user editing and real-time collaboration between students and faculty with G Suite for Education productivity apps like Gmail, Docs, Calendar, and Drive.

Our solutions are designed to be used in multi-cloud and hybrid environments and we have GCP and G Suite services to help you migrate quickly and securely. Start by contacting us or teaming up with a Google Cloud Education partner. Or sign up for a free trial to see how our technology can help you power what’s possible.

K12 Education

Spark learning anytime, anywhere, on any budget.

Help educators find innovative solutions to learning’s biggest barriers with shareable devices and collaborative tools from Google for Education. Versatile Chromebooks equip students for success with fast boot-up time and a world of creative apps. G Suite for Education, which includes productivity apps like Docs, Sheets, and Drive, enables real-time collaboration between students and is available for schools at no cost, with 24/7 support. With Google Classroom, teachers can save time by managing their student communication and assignments with ease.

Our recent impact research, conducted by Evergreen Education Group, found that schools using Chromebooks and G Suite for Education are seeing improved graduation rates, a reduction in the digital divide, and greater innovation in teaching, particularly in STEAM.

See how you can bring the power of technology to your school, no matter the class size or district budget. Getting classrooms set up is simple and you can manage devices and apps from your web browser — manage ten or 10,000 Chromebooks with ease.
