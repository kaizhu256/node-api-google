Financial Services Solutions

Stay competitive and compliant in rapidly changing global markets with Google Cloud’s powerful, scalable technology, including secured multi-cloud and hybrid solutions.

High-performance Compute

Power, scale, and speed for complex financial analysis.

To keep up with massive computing demands, financial institutions like HSBC are moving their data to Google Cloud Platform (GCP). GCP runs on the same infrastructure and private fiber network that powers Google, giving you the scale you need to run training algorithms, portfolio analysis, and risk modeling at the speed of your business. Spin up workloads faster than you can on other clouds, including clusters with a few hundred (or thousand) cores in minutes. Scale up when you need, and then only pay for the compute seconds you use.

Our solutions are easily adaptable for multi-cloud or hybrid environments and we have services to help you migrate quickly and securely. Start by contacting us or teaming up with a Google Cloud partner. Or sign up for a free trial with $300 credit to get your projects up and running quickly.

Analytics & Reporting

Unify fragmented datasets to unlock powerful insights.

Cut across legacy silos and turn decades of public and private data into market and consumer insights with our proven, integrated Big Data solutions. BigQuery, our fully-managed serverless data warehouse, autoscales for performance and runs blazing-fast queries on petabytes of data, getting you insights faster. And because it doesn’t run on VMs and you don’t need clusters to scale, your data stays out of additional silos.

Once your data is analyzed, create custom user-friendly reports with Cloud Data Studio (beta). GCP also seamlessly integrates with third-party visualization tools for real-time exploration of your most complicated data problems. Drive more holistic financial, regulatory, and customer service reporting with our Big Data tools.

Industry-leading ML

Streamline operations and enhance financial modeling.

Investing in a solid AI strategy now can have a significant impact on your business’s future. With Google Cloud’s machine learning services, you can more effectively manage risk, streamline operations, create more personalized customer experiences, improve transaction monitoring, develop better quant models, generate alpha, and more.

Machine learning isn’t as simple as flipping a switch, but with Google Cloud, you don’t need a huge team to get the most out of your data. You can start by taking advantage of proven, pre-trained models like Cloud Vision, Natural Language, and Translate. Or quickly create your own models with Cloud Machine Learning Engine and TensorFlow, one of the most popular machine learning libraries among researchers across the globe. With TensorFlow, you can use our proprietary customer ML accelerator, the Tensor Processing Unit, to run and scale your models. On our production AI workloads that utilize neural network interference, the TPU is 15–30X faster than contemporary GPUs or CPUs.

If you have high-impact machine learning projects, you can partner directly with Google Cloud ML experts at the Advanced Solutions Lab opportunities.

Security & Compliance

We do our part to help you protect your most sensitive data, including customer PII, records, transaction data, and payment card information by offering identity management, network security, and threat detection and response capabilities. To earn your trust, we certify our products against the most rigorous global security and privacy standards, such as ISO 27001, ISO 27017, and ISO 27018; financial control standards, including AICPA SOC and Japan FISC, among others; and industry-specific standards like PCI DSS.
