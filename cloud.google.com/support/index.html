Managed Services

Get Managed Services from a Partner

Managed Service Providers offer hands-on infrastructure, design, implementation, monitoring and support for GCP. These expert partners proactively manage your full day-to-day operations, beyond break/fix resolutions.

Rackspace Managed Services for Google Cloud Platform

Rackspace takes over the intensive day-to-day operations on GCP covering monitoring, logging, patching, backups, system updates and more – all backed by 24x7 hands-on incident response from Google Cloud Platform certified experts. Additionally, Rackspace Managed Services includes access to innovative solutions architects, seasoned deployment engineers and dedicated technical account managers to help you plan, design and secure your Google Cloud architecture and deployment for optimal performance.
