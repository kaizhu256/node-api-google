Ecosystem programs

Google Developers offers a variety of programs for developers, entrepreneurs, business leaders, and community groups worldwide, providing them with support and resources to develop, grow, and earn more with their businesses.

Community-based

Community Groups Program

Includes the GDG platform
Get online training, meetup materials, networking opportunities, and more to help you become a community leader and successfully grow your mobile, web, cloud, or AI developer group. You can also optionally join the GDG platform which offers tailored support to organizers of local groups specifically interested in learning more about Google technologies.
Learn more

GBG

Join a community of business professionals that come together to share knowledge about Google products and other web technologies for business success.
Learn more

Google Developers Experts

Become certified as a developer, product designer, or marketing professional and join the global network of Google Developers Experts. As part of the program, GDEs are invited to exclusive early access programs and to provide strategic feedback to our product teams at Google as representatives of their communities.
Learn more

Women Techmakers

Be a part of global initiatives and programs designed to support and empower women in technology by offering visibility, community, and resources.
Learn more

For startups and agencies

Google Developers Launchpad

Join a global acceleration program that helps startups build and scale great products by matching them with the best of Google - its people, network, and advanced technologies. It includes a six-month accelerator program for top growth-stage startups, a fully-tailored Machine Learning product development studio, and a physical space in San Francisco for training and networking events.
Learn more

Google Developers Agency Program

Apply to receive dedicated training, access to our latest products and APIs, and 1:1 online support so you can deliver high quality Android apps that fit your clients' needs. If your agency already has demonstrated expertise in using Google technologies and a successful track record of building high quality apps, you can also become a Google Developers software development certified agency.
Learn more

Product-specific

Google Cloud Platform Community

Learn how to use Google Cloud Platform services with tutorials submitted by the community and via online forums with contributions from Googlers. You can also join thousands of developers, DevOps, and IT professionals and architects at community meetups to share knowledge, best practices, and insights on using Google Cloud.
Learn more

Google Assistant Developer Community Program

Receive support for your efforts in building great apps for the Google Assistant starting with a $200 monthly Google Cloud credit and a Google Assistant t-shirt when you publish your first app. The perks and opportunities available to you will grow as you hit new milestones.
Learn more
